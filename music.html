Brand New Girl

"BRAND NEW GIRL" is a Japanese song by South Korean girl group Twice. It is the second song from the original Japanese single Candy Pop. The music video for "BRAND NEW GIRL" was released on February 23, 2018.

Yes or Yes

Dance The Night Away

Fancy You

Feel Special

"Feel Special" is a song recorded by South Korean girl group Twice. It is the title track of the extended play of the same name. The single and its music video were released on September 23, 2019.

Likey
